# reaver Toolcard

## Overview
- Summary: Reaver implements a brute force attack against Wi-Fi Protected Setup (WPS) and can recover WPA/WPA2 passphrases.

## Advanced Techniques
- Use only in controlled lab environments.
- Validate lockout protections and stop early on lockout signals.

## Safe Defaults
- Authorized networks only; do not test production networks without written approval.
- Avoid persistent or disruptive actions unless explicitly authorized.

- outputs: evidence.json, findings.json (as applicable)
