# Reporting Validation Checklist

## Required Artifacts
- `report.md` exists in the job output directory.
- `report.json` exists in the job output directory.

## Minimum Content Checks
- Executive summary present.
- At least one finding entry or an explicit "no findings" statement.
- Evidence index populated when evidence files exist.

## Recommended Metadata
- Target and objective populated.
- Generation timestamp present.
