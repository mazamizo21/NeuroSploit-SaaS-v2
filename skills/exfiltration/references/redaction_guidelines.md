# Redaction Guidelines

## Goals
1. Protect sensitive data while preserving proof.
2. Maintain a chain-of-custody note.
3. Record redaction method and operator.

## Safe Handling
1. Mask emails, tokens, and PII.
2. Store raw data only in approved evidence storage.
3. Use consistent redaction markers.

## Evidence Checklist
1. Redaction applied.
2. Storage location recorded.
3. Redaction method notes.
