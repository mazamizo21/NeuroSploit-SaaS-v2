# Exposure Checks

## Goals
1. Identify exposed backups, logs, or configuration files.
2. Record exposure scope and status codes.

## Safe Checks
1. Check for common backup filenames and installer remnants.
2. Inspect robots.txt for sensitive paths.
3. Record redirects and authentication barriers.

## Evidence Checklist
1. Exposed paths and status codes.
2. Notes on sensitive exposure.
3. Response headers or body evidence.
