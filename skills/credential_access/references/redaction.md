# Redaction and Handling

## Goals
1. Preserve evidence while protecting sensitive data.
2. Ensure chain-of-custody notes are captured.
3. Record redaction method and scope.

## Safe Handling
1. Redact credentials and tokens in reports.
2. Store raw data in secure evidence storage only.
3. Use consistent redaction markers in findings.

## Evidence Checklist
1. Redaction applied.
2. Storage location noted.
3. Redaction method and operator identity.
