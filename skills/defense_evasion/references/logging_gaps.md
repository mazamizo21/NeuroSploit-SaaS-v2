# Logging Gaps

## Goals
1. Identify missing or misconfigured logging.
2. Capture evidence without changing settings.
3. Record affected systems and data sources.

## Safe Checks
1. Audit log configuration and retention settings.
2. Confirm log forwarding where applicable.

## Evidence Checklist
1. Logging configuration summary.
2. Gaps and affected systems.
3. Retention settings and forwarding evidence.
