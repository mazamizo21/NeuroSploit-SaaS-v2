# Tamper Protection

## Goals
1. Identify whether tamper protection or self-defense is enabled.
2. Record evidence without changes.
3. Note policy enforcement or override settings.

## Safe Checks
1. Review EDR/AV settings if authorized.
2. Capture tamper protection status.

## Evidence Checklist
1. Tamper protection state.
2. Control coverage notes.
3. Policy enforcement evidence.
