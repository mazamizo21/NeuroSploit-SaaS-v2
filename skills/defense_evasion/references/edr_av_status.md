# EDR and AV Status

## Goals
1. Identify protection coverage and tamper settings.
2. Avoid disabling any controls.
3. Record update status and signature health.

## Safe Checks
1. Query status from system tools or management consoles (read-only).
2. Capture version and update timestamps.

## Evidence Checklist
1. Protection status and version.
2. Tamper protection state.
3. Update status and last update timestamp.
